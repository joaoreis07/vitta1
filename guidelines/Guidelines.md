# Nutricionista Landing Page — Guidelines

## Aesthetic Stance
**Editorial wellness** — Clean, professional, and welcoming. Inspired by wellness editorial design with generous whitespace, clear hierarchy, and trust-building composition.

## Typography
- **Display**: Playfair Display (elegant serif for headings)
- **Body**: Inter (modern sans-serif for readability)
- **Scale**: Clear hierarchy with generous spacing

## Color Palette
Primary wellness palette with natural, calming tones:

- **Primary Green**: `#4A7C59` (forest green — health, nature, growth)
- **Accent Green**: `#6B9D7A` (soft sage — wellness, calm)
- **Warm Beige**: `#F5F1E8` (warm neutral — comfort, approachability)
- **Cream**: `#FDFBF7` (soft white — clean, professional)
- **Text Dark**: `#2C3E2F` (deep green-tinted charcoal)
- **Text Medium**: `#5A6B5C` (muted for secondary text)
- **Text Light**: `#8B9A8D` (subtle for captions)

## Layout Principles
1. **Generous whitespace** — let content breathe
2. **Asymmetric sections** — editorial composition, not rigid grid
3. **Photography integration** — wellness imagery with natural tones
4. **Mobile-first responsive** — seamless phone experience
5. **Clear CTA hierarchy** — WhatsApp conversion focus

## Component Patterns
- **Hero**: Large display heading, professional photo, dual CTAs
- **Section headers**: Serif display with subtle dividers
- **Cards**: Soft shadows, rounded corners, hover lift
- **Buttons**: Primary (green, high contrast), Secondary (outline)
- **Testimonials**: Photo + quote card pattern
- **Before/After**: Side-by-side comparison cards

## Interactions
- Smooth scroll animations on section entry
- Gentle hover states (lift + shadow)
- WhatsApp CTA pulse animation
- Mobile navigation drawer

## Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 768px
- Tablet: 768px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px
